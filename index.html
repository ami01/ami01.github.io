Geospatial Analysis & Modeling

Proficient in advanced spatial analysis techniques, including geostatistics, spatial autocorrelation, and suitability modeling. Expertise in using GIS software (ArcGIS, QGIS) for environmental and hydrological applications.

Remote Sensing & Image Processing

Experience in processing and analyzing satellite imagery (Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS) for land cover mapping, change detection, and environmental monitoring. Skilled in using remote sensing software and techniques (ENVI, ERDAS).

Hydrological Modeling & Water Resource Management

Knowledge of hydrological models (e.g., SWAT, HEC-HMS) for flood forecasting, water balance studies, and climate change impact assessment on water resources. Experience in using geospatial data for hydrological modeling.

Machine Learning & Data Analysis

Proficient in applying machine learning algorithms for classification, regression, and pattern recognition in geospatial and environmental datasets. Skilled in using Python (scikit-learn, TensorFlow) and R for data analysis and ML tasks.

Environmental Modeling & GIS for Disaster Management

Database Management & Scripting
